Frequently Asked Questions

Is NAPS2 really free?

Yes! NAPS2 is completely free and open-source. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium versions. You get full access to all features at no charge.

Will my scanner work with NAPS2?

NAPS2 supports virtually all scanners through WIA and TWAIN protocols. This includes flatbed scanners, document feeders, and network scanners from major brands like Canon, Epson, HP, Brother, and more.

Can I use NAPS2 for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! NAPS2 is licensed under an open-source license that allows both personal and commercial use without any restrictions or licensing fees.

How do I update NAPS2?

NAPS2 will notify you when a new version is available. You can also check for updates within the application or download the latest version from this page.
