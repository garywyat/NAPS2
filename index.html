Powerful Scanning for Everyone

NAPS2 (Not Another PDF Scanner 2) is a completely free and open-source scanning application designed to make document scanning simple and efficient. Whether you're a home user digitizing family photos or a business professional managing important documents, NAPS2 provides all the tools you need.

With support for WIA and TWAIN protocols, NAPS2 works seamlessly with virtually any scanner on the market. The application features an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, customizable profiles, and powerful automation capabilities that streamline your workflow.

Our active community and regular updates ensure you always have access to the latest features and improvements. Join thousands of satisfied users who have made NAPS2 their scanning solution of choice.
