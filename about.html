About NAPS2

Revolutionizing document scanning through open-source innovation

Who We Are

NAPS2 (Not Another PDF Scanner 2) is a leading open-source scanning application that has been helping individuals and businesses digitize their documents for years. We believe that powerful document management tools should be accessible to everyone, which is why NAPS2 is completely free and open-source.

Our software combines professional-grade features with an intuitive interface, making advanced scanning capabilities available to users of all skill levels. From small businesses to large enterprises, NAPS2 serves as a reliable solution for all document digitization needs.

What We Offer

Universal Scanner Support

NAPS2 works seamlessly with virtually any scanner through WIA and TWAIN protocols, ensuring compatibility across different hardware manufacturers and models.

Intelligent Automation

Our advanced features like automatic cropping, deskewing, and batch processing save you time and ensure consistent, professional results every time.

Flexible Output Options

Export your scans in multiple formats including PDF, JPEG, PNG, and TIFF. Create searchable PDFs with built-in OCR technology for easy document retrieval.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Available for various Windows versions including Windows 7, 8, 10, and 11, ensuring you can use NAPS2 regardless of your system configuration.
